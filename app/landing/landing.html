Welcome to Green.

A crowdsourcing platform for bettering the enviroment around you.



What is Green?




Green is a crowdsourcing platform that aims to increase enviromental awareness. Reporting and discovering enviromental issues around you has never been easier. Green aims to bridge the gap between identifying and solving enviromental issues in the community. Through reporting issues, other users will be able to see issues around them and can decide if they would like to resolve that issue.



Report Discover Resolve
With Green, users can report many different enviromental issues around you. Through our web interface, problems that users would like to see be fixed can be reported with just a few clicks. Reports that users enter are avaible to the public. Green can be used to discover the issues around the user. User reports are shown on our map and will show the most common issue in the user's community. Browse the map to see issues close to you or even across the globe. As our database expands, so will your discoveries. Reports are avaible for all users to see. Users can decide to visit the areas with issues and help better the enviroment. Users can group up with other users through Green and work together to improve the enviroment around them.






Green and You.



Green can only continue to grow. As more users join this network, we will have more data points for other users to discover. With your contribution, we can expand our outreach to other people around the world. Together, we can create a greener tomorrow.
